zucchini Pasta

Pasta with zucchini in a garlicky sauce. Serve with crusty bread and a chilled white wine, if desired.

  • 1 pound rotini pasta
  • 5 small zucchini, slice
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2cup grated Parmesan cheese
  1. Step 1 Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain and reserve.
  2. Step 2 Fill a medium sauce pan with lightly salted water. Add zucchini and bring to a boil; boil for 10 minutes or until tender.
  3. step 3 In a large skillet, saute garlic in oil and hot pepper flaker. Add drained zucchini and parsley, then mix all together and simmer for 5 to 10 minutes. Toss with pasta; then add cheese and salt and pepper to taste, and serve.
